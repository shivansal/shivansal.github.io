I am currently an undergraduate student studying computer science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst (2021). While studying, I also work part-time as a software engineer at the Hydrosystems Research Group on campus. Prior to working at the Hydrosystems Group, I joined the Autism Glass Project as a research intern during the summer of 2017 where I got to explore the overlap between machine learning and behavioral therapy for kids with autism. I also served as software engineering intern at Unshackled Ventures during my senior year in high school.

I picked up programming when I was 15 years old and have fallen in love with the craft ever since. I am interested in a variety of topics such as machine learning, operating systems, and algorithms.
